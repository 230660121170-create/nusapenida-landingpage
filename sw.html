Seganing Waterfall

Adventure meets the ocean view.

Seganing Waterfall is one of the most unique spots on the southwest coast of Nusa Penida, known not for its waterfall, but for its breathtaking cliffside panorama. Visitors are treated to sweeping views of the vast ocean framed by rugged limestone cliffs — a sight that captures the raw beauty of the island.

To reach this hidden gem, travelers must descend a steep and challenging trail carved into the cliffside. The path is partially supported by wooden rails and ropes, offering both a thrilling and rewarding adventure for those brave enough to explore it.

At the bottom, you’ll find a small natural pool and the gentle flow of water blending with the sound of crashing waves. Seganing Waterfall is more than just a destination — it’s a journey that rewards you with serenity, thrill, and unforgettable ocean views.
